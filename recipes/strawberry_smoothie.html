Strawberry smoothie

A simple but delicious strawberry smoothie recipe.

Make sure that you wash your hands with soap and clean water before, during and after handling food. Your cooking environment should be clean. Your cooking equipment and tools should be clean.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of greek yoghurt
  • 1 cup of strawberries, washed
  • A tablespoon of ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Put 2 cups of greek yoghurt, 1 cup of strawberries and a tablespoon of ground cinnamon into a blender and blend into a thick paste
  2. Serve into a glass and enjoy!
