Coconut rice recipe

Coconut rice garnished with sesame seeds

This recipe aims to give you a taste of a popular Swahili dish

Make sure that you wash your hands with soap and clean water before, during and after handling food. Your cooking environment should be clean. Your cooking equipment and tools should be clean.

Ingredients

  • 1 mature coconut, washed
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 1 cup of rice, washed
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame seeds, washed
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the coconut. Obtain the copra and coconut water
  2. Put the copra and the coconut water into a blender and blend
  3. Add the resulting mixture and one and a half cups of water into a cooking pot. Bring to boil on medium heat
  4. Once at boiling point, add salt and mix carefully
  5. Add one cup of rice and cover with a lid. Reduce to low heat and cook until all the water has evaporated
  6. Serve and garnish with sesame seeds
