Grilled chicken

Grilled chicken garnished with chopped spring onions(garnishing is optional)

This recipe not only produces tasty chicken but one rich in nutrients.

Make sure that you wash your hands with soap and clean water before, during and after handling food. Your cooking environment should be clean. Your cooking equipment and tools should be clean.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken cut into 4 pieces
  • Salted water (enough to boil the chicken until well cooked)
  • 1 large red onion, chopped
  • 1 spring onion(stalk only), chopped into rings
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 small portion of ginger
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 small bunch of corriander, chopped
  • Freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 4 teaspoons of curry powder in a small clean bowl
  • 1 leveled teaspoon of paprika in a small clean bowl
  • 1 leveled teaspoon of salt in a small clean bowl
  • 4 tablespoons of cooking oil in a clean and airtight dish
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a small clean airtight dish
  • Quarter a cup of drinking water
  • Quarter a cup of clean water

Steps

  1. Boil the pieces of chicken in salted water until cooked. Once cooked, set aside in a clean and airtight container
  2. Pour your quarter cup of drinking water into your blender. Add the curry powder, salt and paprika. Add the chopped mix of vegetables and fruit(red onion, garlic, ginger, tomatoes and corriander) into your blender and blend into a paste.
  3. Pour the paste into the container containing the cooking oil and mix well. Add the orange juice and mix well.
  4. Add your cooked chicken pieces into the mixture above and mix well. Make sure that each piece is well coated.
  5. Grease your grill pan with 1 tablespoon of oil and place it on a cooker on low heat.
  6. Once the pan is hot, add the coated chicken pieces and grill each side until golden brown.
  7. Once golden brown on each side, transfer them on to a clean serving bowl.
  8. Add quarter a cup of clean water to the grill pan. Add the chopped spring onion and steam for two minutes. Use a lid to cover.
  9. Once ready, remove them from the pan and cool
  10. Sprinkle the steamed spring onions gently onto the grilled chicken and serve
