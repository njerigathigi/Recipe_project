Grilled chicken

Grilled chicken garnished with chopped spring onions

This recipe not only produces tasty chicken but one rich in nutrients

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 small portion of ginger
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 small bunch of corriander
  • 4 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 leveled teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 leveled teaspoon of salt
  • 4 tablespoons of cooking oil
